Our Services

🦷

Preventative Care

Routine dental procedures such as cleanings and fluoride treatments aimed at preventing cavities and gum disease.

🔬

Periodontal Care

Management of gum disease through procedures like scaling, root planing, and gum surgery to maintain gum health.

✨

Restoration & Cosmetics

Procedures ranging from dental bonding to implants aimed at repairing damaged teeth and enhancing aesthetics.

🔧

Orthodontics

Treatments such as braces and clear aligners to correct misaligned teeth and improve bite alignment.

🏥

Oral Surgery & Implants

Surgical interventions including extractions, and wisdom teeth removal to address various dental and oral health issues.

😴

Protective Devices

Nightguards to protect teeth from grinding and relieve jaw pains; and sleep apnea appliances to promote better breathing during sleep.

🩺

Endodontic Treatment

Procedures like root canal therapy to treat infected tooth pulp and save the tooth from extraction.

🚨

Emergency Services

Immediate care for urgent dental issues such as severe tooth pain, broken teeth, or oral injuries.

Welcome to Your Dental Fitness Center!

San Gabriel Summit Dental is a family-owned dental office established in 1997 and located in beautiful Downtown Burbank. At San Gabriel Summit Dental, quality and comfort are our top priorities. With over two decades of experience, Dr. Dina M. San Gabriel has been dedicated to delivering exceptional dental services to adults and children.

Dr. San Gabriel provides personalized care, taking the time to understand and address each patient's needs and concerns thoroughly. Keeping patients well-informed is a cornerstone of her practice, ensuring a comfortable and informed experience for all.

About Dr. San Gabriel

Dr. Dina M. San Gabriel has been a cornerstone of Burbank's dental care since founding San Gabriel Summit Dental in 1997. With over two decades of experience, Dr. San Gabriel's commitment to excellence is evident in her gentle, family-oriented approach to dentistry.

Beginning her career with distinction, she ranked among the Top 20 in the 1989 Philippines National Dental Board Exams. Continuously updating her skills with the latest techniques, Dr. San Gabriel ensures her patients receive the highest quality care possible.
